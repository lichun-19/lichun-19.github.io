Pet Travel Platform

Reimagination of the Traveling Experience with Pets | Spring 2021

Introduction

Collaborators | Jing-Hsiang Hsu, Wan-Chi Wang
During our participation in the Design Thinking Workshop at NTU D-School, we applied the design thinking process to a project focused on improving the travel experience of pet owners. This led to the development of a user-centered prototype website designed to make pet-friendly travel easier and more enjoyable.
The platform is designed to reduce preparation time by up to 60% and integrates five key functions, including recommended and customizable itineraries, transportation services, equipment rental, and more.

01 Empathize — Understanding Pet Owners’ Needs
We began by conducting semi-structured interviews (N=3) with pet owners to understand their experiences, frustrations, and unmet needs when traveling with pets. We explored:
  • Background and routines of the owner and pet
  • Difficulties and emotional challenges during trips
  • Causes behind unmet needs and expectations for an ideal travel experience
Using these insights, we created a user persona representing owners who love traveling with their pets but feel overwhelmed by the preparation workload.
02 Define — Insights & Problem Statement
Through affinity diagramming and synthesis, we identified four key needs mentioned across interviews:
  • Keeping pets safe and close during outings
  • Having more pet-friendly transportation options
  • Clear agreements with businesses to allow pets to stay with their owners
  • A simpler way to prepare for overnight trips with pets
A recurring theme was the low “effort-to-happiness ratio (CP 值)”—owners felt the joy of pet travel did not justify the heavy preparation required.

Point of View (POV):
“Pet owners who dream of taking overnight trips with their dogs need a fast and simple preparation method, because balancing effort and joy is essential to them.”
03 Ideate — Exploring Creative Solutions
We used brainstorming and role-playing to generate a diverse range of ideas. After refining and combining concepts, we identified five feasible solution directions:
  • Predicting owner–pet happiness levels for different trip types
  • Centralizing pet-friendly travel information into one platform
  • Offering flexible packaged and customized itineraries
  • Establishing institutions to support pet-owner travel needs
  • Building pet clubs or resorts for dedicated travel experiences
After evaluating feasibility and alignment with our POV, we selected the final design direction:

→ A web platform offering pet-friendly travel recommendations, flexible trip packages, and customizable itineraries tailored to owner and pet needs.

04 Prototype — Four-Page Web Platform
We created a mid-fidelity prototype consisting of four main pages:
① Homepage
  • Recommended pet-friendly travel packages
  • Option to create customized itineraries
  • A short quiz to identify suitable travel styles
② Recommended Trips
  • Filters for budget, stamina, pet energy level, and special needs
  • Star-rated indicators for effort required, happiness, and overall suitability
  • Right-panel detailed itinerary items
  • Drag-and-drop actions for custom itineraries
③ Custom Schedule Builder
  • Visual timeline interface for arranging trip activities
  • Automatic import of selected activities
  • Real-time calculations for owner effort, pet energy, and total cost
④ Equipment Rental & Add-On Services
  • Rental of travel gear such as RVs, bowls, water bottles, and food
  • Optional products like pet party outfits, swimwear, and custom birthday cakes
  • Designed to reduce packing stress and preparation time
05 Test — User Feedback & Insights
Two usability testers evaluated our prototype and provided valuable insights.

Positive Feedback
  • Well-integrated and comprehensive information
  • Strong customization flexibility
  • Significant reduction in time spent researching trips
  • Clear and trustworthy trip recommendations
Suggestions for Improvement
  • Enhance interface intuitiveness
  • Add a real-time online support system
  • Provide optional pet travel insurance
  • Offer professional driver services for pet-friendly transportation
